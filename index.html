Privacy

We respect your privacy. Here's what our extensions collect and why.

General Policy

  • All extensions are open source — you can inspect exactly what data is accessed.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize any user data.
  • No analytics or tracking scripts are included unless explicitly noted.
  • Data stays on your device unless a feature explicitly requires network access.

ibid

The ibid extension may access page content to extract citations and references. This data is processed locally and stored in your browser's local storage.

No remote data collection Local storage only

biopeek

Biopeek inspects biological data structures locally. No data is transmitted to external servers.

No data collection

biogist

Biogist processes documents locally for summarization. No content is sent externally.

No data collection

biokhoj

Biokhoj may query external biological databases to fetch search results. Only search terms are transmitted — no personal data.

Search queries sent externally No personal data
